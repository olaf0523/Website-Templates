Introduction

Hello and welcome to Warped, a dark, fluid template created by Six Shooter Media. Warped is a simple template making use of standard features; a header, a navigation area, and side column, a main column and a footer. Pink and grey make up the colour scheme it the template aesthetics were inspired by the song 'Warped' by the Red Hot Chili Peppers.
