Color Scheme

The various colors options can be applied to text, background, title lines as well as for individual table cells.

1 
Colors

Application groups:

  • Text: txt-xxxxNN (xxxx=color family, NN=color index)
  • Shades: bg-xxxxNN (xxxx=color family, NN=color index)
  • Lines: line-xxxxNN (xxxx=color family, NN=color index)

Basic color families:

  • White: Monocolor)
  • Black: Monocolor)
  • Grey: In increments of 5% - 10%.
  • Yellow: Based on Pantone YellowC. In increments of 10%.
  • Blue: Based on Pantone 2726C. In increments of 10%.
  • Green: Based on Pantone 369C. In increments of 10%
  • Red: Based on Pantone 179C. In increments of 10%
