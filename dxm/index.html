Saturday, December 28, 2002

I have finally finished this latest template. It took me much longer to make this one than my previous designs but it was worth it. I was really trying to make this one of those designs that makes you say "Wow! I want to use that design!"

The stylesheet is very extensive but once you get the hang of it, it should make customizing the colors, fonts, etc. easier. Just don't mess around with things you aren't familiar with, you could unknowingly mess up certain elements of the design.

Saturday, December 28, 2002

Some quick notes:
- When adding a new table in this center column, make sure a line break (<br>) is added inbetween the tables

- Be careful modifying table widths, padding and spacing. It's very easy to throw off the whole design if you aren't sure what you're doing
