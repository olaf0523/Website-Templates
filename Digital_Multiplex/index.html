why use dm?
dm provides a concise user interface, allowing any visitor to your page to navigate quickly and easily.

dm is also designed to allow complete customization. Anyone can add a toolbox, news item or other gadget to the layout without trouble.

dm also allows easy integration of SSI. Headers, footers and content can easily be incorporated into the design.

dm [Digital Multiplex] was a small project I was working on in my spare time during the summer of 2000. I stopped work on it as I became more interested in going to the beach. I am now releasing the design into the hands of the OSWD. Any comments or questions can be directed to Jonathan at x3x93@unb.ca.
